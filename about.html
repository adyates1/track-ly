About

Track-ly is an online playlist generating application whereby groups of people can collaborate in real time to generate Spotify playlists for their parties, it allows people without Spotify accounts to still contribute to the playlist and allows even those with accounts to not have to worry about trying to find the hosts account if they want to generate a playlist

How many times have you been at a party and the music is being run from someones old knackered laptop in the corner of the room, or worst still, it's being played off someones phone who you have to ask permission to use if you want to add a song to the playlist. Track-ly solves aims to solve these issues, as long as people know the party to join - anyone can add songs to the playlist, so you don't have to worry about mashing songs onto someone else's playlist or someone skipping your song that was about to come on next!
