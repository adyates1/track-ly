How to get started!

How do I get started?

You can start by creating a party...

Click ‘Start’ on the homepage. Then type your name and push ‘Create’. You can then confirm party settings – and you’re done!


How do I join a party?

First get the room code...

Once you’ve got the room code, click ‘Start’ on the homepage. Type in your name and room code. Once you’ve clicked ‘Join’, you’ll be launched straight in to your party.


How do I link with Spotify?

In just two clicks...

You can use either of the green buttons in the corner of your party. ‘Import’ / ‘Import existing playlist’ will let Track-ly add to an existing playlist (songs already in the playlist won’t be touched). ‘New’ / ‘New playlist’ will create a brand new playlist in your Spotify account to use with the party.


How do I search for a song?

Once you’re in a party, you can type the song name in to the search box, and then click the search button.


How do I search an album, or all tracks from an artist?

This works similarly to searching for a song. Just type your artist name or album name in to the box, and make sure the search type is set to Album or Artist depending on what you’re looking for. Then click the search button.


How do I delete songs?

Did someone add Nickelback?

Your host needs to have enabled song deletion. If so, a red ‘X’ will next to each song. Click the X to delete the song.
