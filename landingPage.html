Track-ly

No Requests, Just Play!



  • Search any songs from Spotify
  • Add songs to a playlist
  • Give your friends your partys code to join
  • Either create a new playlist or link a playlist
  • Remove or edit your playlist easily
  • Only one person needs a Spotify account to play the playlist

Live playlist editing for your party!

Track-ly is an online playlist generating application whereby groups of people can collaborate in real time to generate Spotify playlists for any occasion. Even if someone doesn’t have a Spotify account, they can still contribute to the playlist, and allows for those with accounts to not have to worry about trying to find the host’s account if they want to generate a playlist.

Anyone can set up a room, and anyone can add to the playlist. All they have to do is enter the unique code, and the collaboration can begin. Songs will be added directly to the Spotify playlist, meaning no more on-the-spot searching for a song or someone skipping the play-queue just to get their song on next.

There are currently NO ads on the app itself, and we'd like you to help us keep it that way! In-order to keep Track-ly afloat (i.e servers, domain name, SSL certificate) we are relying heavily on your donations.

No more asking to request a song!

How many times have you been at a party and the music is being run from someone’s old, knackered laptop in the corner of the room, or worse still, it's being played off of someones phone who you have to ask permission to use if you want to add a song to the playlist? Track-ly aims to solve these issues. As long as people know the party to join, anyone can add songs to the playlist! Now, you don't have to worry about mashing songs onto someone else's playlist or someone skipping your song that was about to come on next!
